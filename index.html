I have been a PhD researcher in Linguistics since September 2021 at the Observatory for Research on Media and Journalism (ORM) and the Center for Natural Language Processing (CENTAL), at UCLouvain (Louvain-la-Neuve, Belgium). My thesis deals with the automatic analysis of subjectivity in French-language press discourse.

My research falls more broadly within the field of computational social sciences. In particular, I am interested in the study of social biases in large data corpora, the integration of artificial intelligence into different writing processes (journalistic, scientific, literary) and narratology.

Since 2022, I have been an active member of the COST Action "What are Opinions?" (CA21129) alongside a large number of European researchers.
